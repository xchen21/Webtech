Current Sports Events

Beijing Sinobo Guoan Soccor Game


Beijing Sinobo Guoan F.C. is a professional Chinese football club that currently participates in the Chinese Super League under license from the Chinese Football Association. The team is based in the Chaoyang District in beijing and their home stadium is the Worker's Stadium.
